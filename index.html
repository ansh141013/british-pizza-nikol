Welcome to British Pizza Nikol – Ahmedabad

British Pizza Nikol is Ahmedabad's premier all-you-can-eat pizza and salad destination, located in the heart of Nikol at 125 Town Plaza. We bring you the best deal in town — enjoy our unlimited buffet with 51+ varieties of freshly made pizzas, garlic breads, hot and cold soups, a sprawling salad bar, pasta, and irresistible desserts.
